💡 Supported Notation:
Basics: Use * multiply · / divide · ^2 power · sqrt(...) square root · cbrt(...) cube root
Calculus: diff(v, t) = dv/dt · int(F, s) = ∫F ds · also accepts dv/dt and ∂v/∂t directly
Trig: sin(omega*t) · cos(k*x) · arctan(v/c) — argument must be dimensionless
Log/Exp: ln(N) · log(P/P0) · exp(E/kT) · e^(x) — argument must be dimensionless
Hyperbolic: sinh(x) · cosh(x) · tanh(x) — dimensionless argument
Misc: abs(x) or |x| preserves dimension · e^(...) auto-converts to exp(...)
⚠ Key rule: Only sqrt and cbrt can take dimensional arguments. All other math functions require dimensionless arguments.
